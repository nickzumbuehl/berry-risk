What happened

Frost in spring. Drought through summer. Record heat at harvest.

Serbia is the world's top raspberry exporter. In 2025, three separate weather events hit in quick succession — each making the damage from the last one worse. By the time prices moved, most buyers had already missed it.

March 2025
Spring Frost

Temperatures fell to −7°C right as the plants came out of dormancy. The timing couldn't have been worse — up to half the national crop was gone before the season had even started.

See frost data
May – July 2025
Record Drought

Seven weeks without meaningful rain through the core growing period. The soil dried out, plants stressed, and most Serbian raspberry growers have no irrigation — there was nothing they could do.

See drought data
June – July 2025
Extreme Heat

Then 38–41°C during harvest — the hottest on record. For plants already weakened by seven weeks without rain, it was the end.
